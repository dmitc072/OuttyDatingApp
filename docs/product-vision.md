# Product Vision — Outty

## Product Overview

Outty is a mobile application that helps people connect with others who enjoy the same outdoor activities. Users are matched based on shared interests, skill level, and location, making it easier to find compatible adventure partners for activities such as hiking, kayaking, climbing, and backpacking.

---

## Far Vision

Outty aims to become a trusted platform for connecting outdoor enthusiasts. In the future, the application may expand to include:

- Group adventure planning
- Verified guides and instructors
- Gear-sharing between users
- Outdoor community groups
- Partnerships with parks and outdoor brands

The long-term goal is to create a community where people can safely discover, plan, and enjoy outdoor adventures together.

---

## Near Vision

By the end of this semester, the team will deliver a working mobile prototype that allows users to:

- Sign in using Google Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Create and manage a user profile
- Select adventure interests and skill level
- View and match with compatible users
- Exchange messages after a successful match

The project will focus on delivering a reliable minimum viable product (MVP). Features such as group matching, social media integration, voice/video communication, and equipment sharing are outside the scope of this semester.
